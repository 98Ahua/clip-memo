## 2. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
### 1. My content/data is missing, how can I recover it?
ClipMemo is a completely local offline application. We do not operate any servers, and cannot access or store any of your data.
If you have NOT enabled iCloud sync: Your data is only stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app, device formatting, or system restore will cause permanent deletion of local data, which cannot be recovered. Please manually back up important content regularly.
If you have enabled iCloud sync: You can manually trigger sync in the app, or go to iOS System Settings - Apple ID - iCloud to confirm that iCloud permission for ClipMemo is enabled, and your data will be restored after the system sync is completed.
### 2. Face ID unlock cannot be enabled / is not working?
Please confirm that your device supports Face ID, and you have completed face enrollment in iOS System Settings - Face ID & Passcode.
Go to iOS System Settings - ClipMemo, and confirm that Face ID permission is enabled.
If it still does not work, you can restart the app or your device and try again. This function is a system-level capability, and we cannot obtain or modify any of your biometric information.
### 3. The built-in keyboard cannot be used / does not appear?
Go to iOS System Settings - General - Keyboard - Keyboards, and confirm that the ClipMemo keyboard has been added.
Go to iOS System Settings - ClipMemo, and confirm that keyboard-related permissions are enabled.
If it still does not work, you can restart the app or your device and try again. All keyboard data is processed locally only, and no content will be uploaded.
### 4. After purchasing paid features, I cannot unlock the benefits / restore purchase failed?
Please confirm that the Apple ID you are using is the same account used for the purchase.
Ensure your device has a normal network connection, go to the paid page in the app, and click the Restore Purchase button, the system will automatically sync your purchase status.
All transactions are processed by the official Apple App Store. We cannot obtain or modify your order information. If you still have problems, you can contact Apple official customer service to verify the order status.
### 5. How to cancel my subscription / turn off auto-renewal?
All subscription services are managed by Apple officially. You can operate through the following steps:
Open iOS System Settings, tap your Apple ID at the top
Select Subscriptions, find the subscription item corresponding to ClipMemo
Tap Cancel Subscription and complete the operation according to the system prompts
After canceling the subscription, you can still use the paid benefits until the end of the current subscription period.
### 6. Can I recover my data after uninstalling the app?
Without iCloud sync enabled: Uninstalling the app will delete all local data at the same time, which cannot be recovered.
With iCloud sync enabled: After reinstalling the app, enable iCloud permission, and your data will be restored after the system sync is completed.
### 7. iCloud sync is not working?
Confirm that your Apple ID is logged into iCloud normally, and your device has a normal network connection.
Go to iOS System Settings - Apple ID - iCloud, confirm that iCloud Drive is enabled, and the sync permission for ClipMemo is turned on.
Confirm that your iCloud storage space is sufficient. Insufficient storage space will cause sync failure.
### 8. How to enable and use the Continuous Copy function?
The Continuous Copy function requires you to enable the Allow Full Access permission for the keyboard first. The specific operation and usage steps are as follows:
Go to iOS System Settings - General - Keyboard - Keyboards - ClipMemo, and turn on the Allow Full Access switch;
Bring up the ClipMemo keyboard, tap the menu button in the top-left corner of the keyboard, and select the Continuous Copy option;
After entering the Continuous Copy page, the function will be automatically enabled, and you can use the relevant capabilities of Continuous Copy normally.
*Note: Even if the Allow Full Access permission is enabled, the ClipMemo keyboard will not record, upload, or collect any of your input content or personal information. All data is processed only locally on your device, in strict compliance with our Privacy Policy.
### 9. How to use the Word Segmentation Selection function?
Bring up the ClipMemo keyboard and switch to the Clipboard list page;
Long-press the clipboard content card you need to process, and the word segmentation selection menu will pop up automatically;
Tap the Word Segmentation Selection option in the menu to enable the function, and perform word segmentation processing and selection operations on the content.
