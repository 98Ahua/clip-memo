# ClipMemo Privacy Policy
**Last updated: March 2026**

Thank you for choosing to use **ClipMemo**! We deeply understand the importance of privacy to you, so this application always takes protecting your data security as the top priority. This Privacy Policy aims to clearly and transparently explain how we handle your information, the way data is stored, and the user rights you possess. Please read this policy carefully before using the app.

## 1. Data Storage and Server
**This application does not build, operate, or use any external servers.**
All your data is stored only on your local device, or synced to **Apple iCloud** only when you actively enable it.
We will not upload any data to our own servers or third-party servers.

## 2. Information We Collect
We only collect and store the following non-personal information to ensure the app functions properly:
- **Subscription status**: To determine whether you have access to premium features (via Apple's In-App Purchase system).

## 3. Data Storage Location
All your data is stored exclusively in:
- **Your local device storage**
- **Apple iCloud (only when you actively enable iCloud sync)**

We **cannot access, view, obtain, or use any of your data** at any time.

## 4. About the App's Built-in Keyboard
ClipMemo includes an **in-app built-in keyboard** to provide a more convenient input experience:
- The keyboard **does not record your input history**
- The keyboard **does not upload any input content**
- The keyboard **does not collect any of your personal information**
- All keyboard-related data is processed locally on the device with no internet connection required.

## 5. User Control Over Data
You have **complete control** over your data:
- You can view, edit, and delete any content at any time
- You can clear all local data with one click
- You can turn iCloud sync on or off in your device settings
- Uninstalling the app will automatically delete all data stored locally by the app
- You can delete synced data through your iCloud settings at any time

## 6. Face ID / Biometric Authentication
If you use Face ID to unlock the app:
- It is only used for local security verification
- Biometric information **never leaves your device**
- We **will not obtain, save, or upload any biometric data**

## 7. In-App Purchases and Payments
All paid features, permanent versions, and subscription services of the app are processed entirely through **Apple's official In-App Purchase (IAP) system**.
- We will not obtain any of your payment information, order details, or bank card information
- All transactions are handled by Apple; we only receive the purchase status.

## 8. User Rights
As a user, you have the following rights:
- The right to access all data stored locally or in iCloud
- The right to modify, delete, and clear all your content
- The right to choose whether to use iCloud sync
- The right to uninstall the app and delete all data at any time
- The right to ask questions and make suggestions regarding this privacy policy

## 9. Third-Party Services
This app **does not integrate any third-party SDKs, advertisements, statistics, tracking, analytics, or push services**.
We will not share, disclose, or provide your data to any third parties.

## 10. Updates to This Privacy Policy
We may occasionally revise this Privacy Policy due to feature updates. The "Last updated" date will be updated upon revision. Material changes will be prompted within the app.
Your continued use of ClipMemo constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
